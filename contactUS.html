Contact Us

Our Contact Center is ready and willing to help out. If you need to talk to an operator please call 899.999.897 (info and prices at the bottom of the page).

2018/19 season-ticket holders and Juventus Members can call (+39) 011.45.44.095 or (+39) 011.07.23.021 Monday to Friday from 10:00 to 20:00 or Saturday from 10:00 to 14:00, excluding holidays. The service is only available within Italy and calls are charged at the local or national rate set by your phone provider. Season-ticket holders must enter their Supporters’ Card number while Juventus Members enter the Membership number printed on their Membership Card. J1897 Members and Black&White/Premium Members can also find their Membership number on the My Profile page after logging in to Juventus.com.

Fans calling from abroad can call (+39) 011.45.30.486 (Monday to Friday from 10:00 to 20:00 or Saturday from 10:00 to 14:00, excluding holidays). Calls are charged at the rate set by your phone provider. Otherwise you can fill out a form explaining the reasons behind your query (available for registered users).
